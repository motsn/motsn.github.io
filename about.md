A blog about football statistics, written and developed by [Dom Christie](http://www.domchristie.co.uk).

Football data is sourced from [Football-Data.co.uk](http://www.football-data.co.uk). Graphs are drawn with [D3.js](http://d3js.org). The logo font is [Monoton Regular](https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Monoton) by Vernon Adams. The site has been created with [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com), and is hosted on GitHub pages. Its source code, including the articles, can be found on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/motsn/motsn.github.io). Feedback, corrections, and contributions are welcome.
